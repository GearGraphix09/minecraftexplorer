Frequently Asked Questions

What are biomes in Minecraft? +
Biomes are different environmental regions in Minecraft that have unique terrain, plants, animals, and weather.
How do I find a specific biome? +
You can explore the world or use maps and seeds to locate biomes. Some mods also help in finding biomes.
Can I build anything special in different biomes? +
Yes! Different biomes inspire different builds and aesthetics. For example, a snowy castle or a desert temple.
