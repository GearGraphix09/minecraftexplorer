Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Minecraft biome?

+

Minecraft biomes are regions in the game world with distinct geography, climate, and resources.

Can I visit all biomes in one world?

+

Yes, biomes are spread across the Minecraft world and can be explored by traveling.

Are all biomes safe?

+

Some biomes like the Nether contain dangerous mobs, so be prepared before exploring.
